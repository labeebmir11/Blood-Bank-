About LifeStream

LifeStream is a comprehensive blood bank management system designed to connect donors with recipients through a network of hospitals and healthcare professionals. Our mission is to ensure that blood is available to those in need, when they need it.

By streamlining the blood donation and request process, we help save lives and make the healthcare system more efficient. Our platform connects donors, recipients, doctors, and hospitals in a secure and user-friendly environment.

Our Services

For Doctors

Manage blood inventory, process requests, and keep track of donations at your hospital.

For Donors

Register as a donor, find donation centers, and track your donation history.

For Recipients

Request blood when needed and get notified when matching donors are available.
